Winter holidays report, or “Namaste” from Nepal!

Some of you may know that during the last winter holidays, I set aside my violin. Instead of hard practice and preparations for upcoming events and recordings, I packed my things and went to Nepal for almost two weeks. I aimed to visit three places: Kathmandu (the capital), Lumbini (Buddha’s birthplace) and Pokhara, from where I went to the Himalayas for my first four days of trekking. I don’t have a degree in storytelling, so I prefer to share with you the photos I took on the way there and back….
